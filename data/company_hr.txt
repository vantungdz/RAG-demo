Company HR Policy Handbook

1. Working Hours
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are entitled to a 1-hour lunch break.

2. Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

3. Leave Policy
Employees receive:
- 12 days annual leave
- 10 public holidays
- 5 sick leave days

Unused annual leave can be carried forward for a maximum of 5 days into the next year.

4. Performance Review
Performance reviews occur twice a year:
- Mid-year review in June
- Year-end review in December

5. Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect diversity, and comply with workplace safety regulations.
