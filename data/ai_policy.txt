AI Usage Policy – Internal Guidelines

This document describes the company policy for using Artificial Intelligence tools internally.

1. Purpose
AI tools such as large language models can be used to assist with drafting documents, generating code, and summarizing information. However, AI outputs must always be reviewed by a human before publication.

2. Data Privacy
Employees must NOT input confidential customer data, personal identifiable information (PII), or proprietary algorithms into public AI services.

3. Code Generation
AI-generated code must go through:
- Code review
- Security scan
- Performance testing

4. Decision Making
AI tools are not allowed to make final business decisions. They may provide recommendations, but a human must approve all strategic decisions.

5. Logging and Monitoring
All AI-assisted workflows must be logged for auditing purposes.

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action.
